I am a postdoctoral research scientist in the Dept. of of Computer Science at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT).
I completed my Ph.D., M.S., and B.S. in Information Systems Science at Soka University, Tokyo, Japan.

Research Interests

  • Distributed Systems: file systems, replication, clusters
  • Mobile Ad-hoc Networking: energy-efficient broadcasting, distributed emulation architecture, wireless localization
  • Opportunistic Computing: spatio-temporal content dissemination, spatio-temporal service sharing
  • Network Analysis: mobility analysis
  • Graph Theory: distributed graph algorithms,
